Momo

Momo is a traditional Nepali dumpling filled with juicy meat or vegetables, wrapped in a soft dough and steamed to perfection. Served with a spicy tomato achar, it's a favorite comfort food across Nepal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • Minced buff/chicken or mixed vegetables
    •
  • Finely chopped onion, garlic, and ginger
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Salt, pepper, and spices to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour with water to make a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling by mixing the minced meat or vegetables with onion, garlic, spices, and soy sauce.
  3. Roll out small dough circles, add filling, and fold into dumpling shapes.
